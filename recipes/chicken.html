Crispy Fried Chicken

This authentic Fried Chicken is a delicious and tempting appetizer recipe, which is prepared with chicken, dipped in buttermilk and coated in a paste of all-purpose flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Salt and pepper chicken pieces to taste
  3. Roll in flour to coat
  4. Place chicken pieces in skillet and fry on medium heat until one side is golden brown
  5. Turn and brown other side until chicken is no longer pink inside and its juices run clear
  6. Drain on paper towel and serve!
